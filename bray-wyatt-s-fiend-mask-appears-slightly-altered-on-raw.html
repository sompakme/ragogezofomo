Bray Wyatts Fiend Mask Appears Slightly Altered On RAW

Bray Wyatt’s Fiend is one of the most popular incarnations within the WWE Universe in a very long time. The Fiend’s mask is a work of art and totally original as well. WWE is also making a pretty penny off of selling replicas to fans, however, there might be two designs for this already famous mask.

The Fiend wrestled at Summerslam in a brutal match against Finn Balor. The beating was so great that Balor took long holiday afterward. If you compare these photos from our Ringside News social media account, it appears Wyatt was wearing a different mask this week on RAW.

It looks like the mask The Fiend was wearing on RAW differed from the darker one he had on at Summerslam. We would love to know why it appears to be so different. At first, we thought perhaps it was the way the light is hitting the masks. Upon close examination it does appear that the one on RAW was a bit brighter and “cleaner” to a certain extent.

The mask is still awesome and Bray Wyatt is absolutely owning this new character. It’s no wonder why Wyatt said it saved his career, because we can’t wait to see what’s next for the Firefly Fun House and The Fiend.
