Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas Give 2 Different Takes on Falls It Jacket

Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas's latest joint appearance will have you falling for their outerwear.

The fashionable couple stepped out in New York City yesterday, each showing off their style in two of this season's trending jackets.

The Game of Thrones alum opted for a sophisticated belted trench coat, this cotton drill iteration hailing from WARDROBE.NYC. The coat includes shoulder epaulettes, a rain guard, a buckled belt, and tortoiseshell buttons. She layered the statement piece over a black top, black trousers, and black riding boots from Khaite. To accessorize, she wore rectangular sunglasses, also in black, from Los Angeles–based eyewear brand Akila.

Meanwhile, Jonas provided a complementary contrast to his wife's classic look. The singer wore an all-black ensemble, consisting of faded black straight-leg jeans, a printed black button-down, and black boots. He stayed warm in a black leather motorcycle jacket, which featured a deep brown fur-trim collar and multicolored patches along the sleeves and chest. For a vintage touch, Jonas finished the look with a pair of '70s-esque red-framed sunglasses with an amber tint.

James Devaney//Getty Images

The power couple is known to always dress to the nines, whether it's on the red carpet or on the street.

The two enjoyed a night out together at last month's Academy Museum of Motion Pictures Gala in Los Angeles, where they again made a fashion statement via their outerwear.

At the time, Turner wore a long Louis Vuitton suede blazer with tan and maroon panels, which she styled with a leather miniskirt and black lace stockings. Jonas also brought his own flair to the star-studded event, wearing a fitted leather blazer and matching leather trousers, also from the French fashion house. The set was emblazoned with a graphic illustration print showcasing a grommet buckle strap.
