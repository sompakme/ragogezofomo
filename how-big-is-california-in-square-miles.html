California: How Big is This Golden Gigantic State?

Ah, California. The land of sunshine, movie stars, and enough avocados to keep a millennial happy for a lifetime. But have you ever stopped to wonder, just how big is this behemoth of a state?

Bigger Than Your Backyard, But Smaller Than Your Dreams (Maybe)

California boasts a whopping 163,695 square miles (423,967 square kilometers). That's a lot of land, folks. Like, enough space to fit Texas and New York combined, with room leftover for a few Hollywood parties.

Fun Fact: If California were a pizza, it would be the kind you need a forklift to move. You'd probably need a nap after just one slice (of state, not pizza).

California vs. The World: A Totally Serious Size Showdown (Kind Of)

  • California vs. France: California puts the "French" in "French fries" when it comes to size. California is actually bigger than France.  Take that, croissants!

  • California vs. The United Kingdom: The Queen might need a bigger map because California is also larger than the entire United Kingdom. Sorry, not sorry, corgis. 

  • California vs. Your Average Fridge: Okay, this isn't exactly a world power throwdown, but let's be honest, California is definitely bigger than your fridge. Unless you have one of those industrial-sized monstrosities that needs its own zip code.

So, What Can You Do With 163,695 Square Miles?

  • Get Lost (But Hopefully Find Your Way Back): With all that space, you could spend a lifetime exploring California's diverse landscapes, from the towering redwoods to the sun-drenched beaches. Just don't forget your phone charger, because cell service can be spotty in the wilderness (unless you're a millionaire with a private satellite network).

  • Start Your Own Country (We Won't Stop You): With enough resources and maybe a catchy national anthem, you could theoretically carve out your own little nation-state within California. Just be prepared for some resistance from the existing residents who might not be too keen on a new king (or queen) on the block.

  • Hide a Really Big Secret (But We Wouldn't Recommend It): All that space means there are plenty of places to bury...stuff. But let's just say, California has enough earthquakes already. Maybe avoid any unnecessary digging projects of questionable legality.

California's size is truly something to behold. It's a state that offers endless possibilities, from chasing Hollywood dreams to simply finding a quiet corner to soak up the sun. So next time you're thinking about California, remember, it's not just big, it's yuge (and yes, we went there).
