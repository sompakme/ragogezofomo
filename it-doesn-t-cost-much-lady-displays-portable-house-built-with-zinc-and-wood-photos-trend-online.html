&quot;It Doesn't Cost Much&quot;: Lady Displays Portable House Built with Zinc and Wood, Photos Trend Online

  • A lady has garnered attention on social media after sharing pictures of a unique house made entirely of zinc and wood
  • Many netizens applauded the resourcefulness of the owner in building such a unique and beautiful home
  • Others expressed concerns about the heat level inside the house as a result of the zinc used for the design

In a recent post on X, a hardworking Nigerian lady showcased a uniquely designed new house.

Taking to the X app, the lady identified as @giftvitaliss showed off the house built with only zinc and wood.

Lady's unconventional home built with zinc and wood

The space included a bedroom, a kitchen, and an empty area, making it a truly one-of-a-kind dwelling.
