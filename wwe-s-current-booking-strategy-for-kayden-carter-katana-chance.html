WWEs Current Booking Strategy For Kayden Carter &amp; Katana Chance

Kayden Carter and Katana Chance were called up to the WWE main roster during the 2023 Draft, and their names surprised some fans. The former NXT Women’s Tag Team Champions have a lot of experience together as a team, and they are legit best friends outside the ring, but a lot of WWE fans might not have seen what they can really do yet.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE RAW this week.

Katana Chance and Kayden Carter defeated Chelsea Green and Sonya Deville in a tag team match during WWE RAW this week. That women’s match was a nice addition to the card, and there is a reason for that as well.

During Wrestling Observer Radio, Dave Meltzer stated that WWE wants to get more television time for Kayden Carter and Katana Chance. The two have a lot of experience, but they need that exposure to a greater audience.

Kayden Carter and Katana Chance just need wins. They need people to know who they are. They need some TV time. At least they were on the show. They could have not been on the show, easily.

During WWE RAW this week, BWE also tweeted out that the build for Kayden Carter and Katana Chance is building. The proven WWE insider noted that the build started this week.

Kayden Carter and Katana Chance both have accomplished backgrounds in sports outside WWE. Carter was a top NCAA basketball player, and Chance was made famous for her run with American Ninja Warrior. Only time will tell what they can do in WWE.

We will have to see if a Women’s Tag Team Title run is in Kayden Carter and Katana Chance’s future on the WWE main roster. Right now, those titles have a unification situation to undergo first.
