Rita Ora says she's prepared to face her critics on The Voice

    Rita Ora has said that she's prepared to face her critics as she makes her debut on The Voice this weekend. The singer revealed that she'd be lying if she said she didn't pay attention to criticism on Twitter, but she tries not to take it to heart. "Anytime I go on Twitter there's always the good, but the bad as well," Rita said. "I try to just blank out what I don't want and take in what I wanna keep. I'm ready for the ups and downs, but for me there's only ups. The 24-year-old also revealed that she plans to be an "open book" on the BBC show.
