Hoda Kotb's Today absence continues - where is the Today with Jenna and Hoda star?

The Today show host has been absent from Today since Friday

    Hoda Kotb has kept fans confused and concerned as she remained absent from her usual role co-hosting theToday Showon Monday morning.

    Hoda was also absent on Friday, when she was replaced by previous anchor Tom Llamas.

    The popular news anchor was replaced this time by Craig Melvin, who is usually co-host for the third hour of as well as a and MSNBC News anchor and reporter. See Al Roker joke with Craig in the video below.
