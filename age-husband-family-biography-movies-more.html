Age, Husband, Family, Biography, Movies &amp; More

Swaralipi Chatterjee (born April 15, 1988) is a Bengali actress, entrepreneur, model, owner, and founder of a coffee shop from West Bengal, India. She rose to fame for her incredible acting in TV series and movies. She has also worked in some commercials and televisions.

Chatterjee has opened her own coffee shop named Abar Baithak located in Hyderabad. She has promoted many people through her social media accounts. Many singers and musicians visit her shop for live performances. 

Biography

Swaralipi Chatterjee was born on April 15, 1988, in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. She is currently living in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Her zodiac sign as per birthdate is Aries.

She has completed her education at a local school in Kolkata and later got admission to a reputed college for further education. She has not disclosed much about her qualifications to the public.

Husband, Marriage, and Children 

Swaralipi Chatterjee was working in a TV series named Agnipariksha with co-actor Shoumo Banerjee. They were popularly known as the Rudra and Doel, because of their characters in the series. They talked to each other for a while and Swaralipi fell in love with him. 

It’s been more than three months of their friendship. Swaralipi proposed him for marriage and Shoumo accepts. Actress Swaralipi Chatterjee privately married her husband Shoumo Banerjee in early 2017 in presence of the family members only. Rest is secret and not revealed by them. They have a daughter named Putuli, who is 3-4 years old as of May 2021. 

After some time of marriage, Swaralipi and Shoumo Banerjee start living separately. The reason for their distance is not known. In an interview, Swaralipi Chatterjee shared that she is a single mother and manages everything alone. 

Career – As an Actress

Swaralipi Chatterjee started her career as an actress in 2008 with a romantic Bengali movie named ‘Eti’. It was directed by Sambit Nag and Sumana Mukherjee. She played the lead role of Ananya along with Bhaswar Chatterjee and Manajit Boral. The movie was a hit and people liked her acting very much. 

In 2012, she worked in a TV series named Checkmate which was created by Mitali Bhattacharya. The stars of the series were Amitabh Bhattacharjee, Saurabh Chatterjee, and Aparajita Ghosh Das. She acted as Ritu in this series. Later, Swaralipi featured in a movie named ‘Chaar’. Sandip Ray was the director and the other casts were Rajatabha Dutta, Paran Banerjee, and Saswata Chatterjee.
