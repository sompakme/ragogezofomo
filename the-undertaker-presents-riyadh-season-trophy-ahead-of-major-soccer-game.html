The Undertaker Presents Riyadh Season Trophy Ahead of Major Soccer Game

The Undertaker’s dedication to putting his body on the line for fans’ enjoyment has earned him legendary status, though it comes with its drawbacks. He is known to make some random appearances from time to time and it appears The Deadman is now in Saudi Arabia, where he presented the Riyadh Season Trophy ahead of a big soccer game.

The WWE Hall of Famer officially put an end to his in-ring career at Survivor Series in 2020. His final match occurred at WrestleMania 36 the same year, where he emerged victorious in a Boneyard match against AJ Styles.

The Phenom made his way to Saudi Arabia today, as he went there to present the Riyadh Season Trophy before the game between Al Nassar & Al Hilal. Football icon Cristiano Ronaldo was also looking on while The Deadman presented the trophy.

The Undertaker previously mentioned facing accusations of ruining people’s childhoods. Nonetheless, fans always love the fact that The Undertaker has a sense of humor and that’s unlikely to change anytime soon.
