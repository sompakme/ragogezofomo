Karen Laine’s Early Life and Career

Karen Laine was born and raised in the United States. She initially pursued a career in law, working as a defense attorney. However, her passion for home renovation led her to switch careers. In 2007, she and her daughter Mina founded Two Chicks and a Hammer, a home renovation business based in Indianapolis. Their work caught the attention of HGTV, leading to the creation of “Good Bones.”

Good Bones: A Major Source of Income

“Good Bones” premiered in 2016 and quickly gained popularity. The show follows Karen and Mina as they renovate homes in their hometown. The success of “Good Bones” has significantly contributed to Karen’s net worth, with the show now in its sixth season.

Two Chicks and a Hammer

Two Chicks and a Hammer, the home renovation business Karen co-founded with her daughter, is another significant source of her wealth. The company focuses on revitalizing neighborhoods by rehabilitating homes that are in desperate need of repair.

Real Estate Investments

Aside from the income generated from the TV show and their business, Karen and Mina also profit from the sale of the homes they renovate. These real estate investments have proven to be lucrative, further boosting Karen’s net worth.

Karen Laine’s Retirement

In 2019, Karen announced her retirement from Two Chicks and a Hammer, though she continues to appear on “Good Bones.” Despite stepping back from the day-to-day operations of the business, her previous work and ongoing TV appearances continue to contribute to her net worth.

Public Speaking and Appearances

Karen’s fame has led to opportunities for public speaking and appearances at home and garden shows, which provide an additional source of income. Her expertise in home renovation and her charismatic personality make her a sought-after speaker in the industry.

Merchandising

Future Projects
