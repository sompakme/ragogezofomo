10 Best Loft Venues For Rent in Washington, DC

Industrial themed weddings are popular choices for contemporary couples. These venues feature exposed brick walls, original factory windows, and high ceilings lit with light strings. Most major cities have long histories as former industrial hubs, and Washington, D.C. is no exception. Buildings that were once a part of factories, warehouses, and department stores have now given way to affordable, flexible event spaces!
Many industrial lofts are blank slates where you can include any elements you wish. Others come with elements like chic contemporary furniture, side rooms, polished hardwood floors, and audio-visual equipment installed for weddings, social mixers, or product launch events. A lucky few industrial lofts are multi-story units where you'll have rooftop access for dancing or banquet halls for fine dining.
