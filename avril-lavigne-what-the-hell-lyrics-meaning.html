So, many people mention her actual relationship, or they think she's in a bad relationship and broke it off because she deserves better.
I only saw one comment that says something else, and it's what I first interpreted myself:

She cheated. She was making out with his friend and they were STILL TOGETHER:
"All cause I was making out with your friend."
"You're on your knees, begging 'please
Stay with me'
But honestly
I just need to be a little crazy"

Avril WAS making out with his/her friend, yet he/she IS begging to stay with her. That means they hadn't split yet, but she already made out with the best friend. All this with an upbeat tone and glorifying sleeping around, which is absolutely fine, IF SHE'S SINGLE.

I acknowledge that it might be that she was in a bad relationship and just wanted to split and go "a little crazy". But if that's the case then there is a glaring error in tense between those two lines. But I don't think that's what is happening here.

Cheating is wrong, always.
