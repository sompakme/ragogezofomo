See Hailey Bieber Pair a Leather Coat with a Tiny Skirt and Tights in NYC

Hailey Bieber couldn't help but look effortlessly cool during her latest NYC outing.

The model was spotted out solo yesterday, in a new iteration of a classic style paring: the oversized jacket with tiny bottoms. For the paparazzi run in, she styled a large leather bomber over a black sweater, layered with a white shirt underneath. She finished the look with a pair of sheer tights and a leather miniskirt that was so short it gave the illusion of a no-pants look on first glance.

Her accessories added a preppy vibe to the ensemble, including white crew socks, chunky black loafers, a shiny shoulder bag, and thin rectangular shades.

Getty Images

The Rhode founder has spent the past week showing off her enviable style all over NYC, with a quick jaunt up to Toronto for a Tiffany & Co. event. Her off-duty looks have trended toward leather and oversized coats, as well as other super-short miniskirts.

For a low-key dinner with husband Justin Bieber, the model layered on two leather jackets, one an oversized leather blazer and the other a sleek biker jacket. She wore the coats over a gray sweater with a white collar peeking underneath, and opted for a tiny black miniskirt to balance out her silhouette. She finished the look with shiny black wedge pumps, rectangular shades with a cobalt blue tint, and a light blue mini bag from Bottega Veneta.

The couple was also spotted on a coffee run last weekend, where Hailey embraced the preppy Y2K aesthetic in a long black belted coat from Saint Laurent, a beige collared knit sweater, a gray pleated skirt from Misbhv, and chunky leather loafers from Proenza Schouler. She accessorized with sheer Calzedonia tights, white crew socks, black shades, and a cream Bottega Veneta shoulder bag.

Getty Images

Hailey spoke on about her philosophy for dressing up for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story.

"I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself," she said. "Even if I'm just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!"
