His association with the movie industry, his relationship status, and his notable collaborations have garnered attention. Here’s an insight into Karnesh Sharma’s life, family, relationships, and career:

Early Life and Education:

  • Karnesh Sharma’s exact date of birth is not available.
  • He hails from Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India, and grew up in Dehradun before settling in Mumbai.
  • His educational background includes schooling at Army School.

 

Career and Achievements:

  • Karnesh Sharma ventured into the film industry as a producer and director.
  • His journey began with a stint in the Merchant Navy.

Tripti Dimri Relationship and Career:

Facts about Karnesh Sharma:
