In addition to cinema and television roles, Estelle Scher-Gettleman (July 25, 1923 – July 22, 2008), best known by her stage name Estelle Getty, was an American actress who performed in theatre, film, and television roles.  In addition to her performance as Sophia Petrillo on The Golden Girls from 1985 to 1992, which earned her an Emmy and a Golden Globe, she also appeared on The Golden Palace from 1992 to 1993 and Empty Nest from 1993 to 1995, both of which earned her an Emmy and a Golden Globe.  She struggled from Lewy body dementia in her latter years, after she had retired from performing.
