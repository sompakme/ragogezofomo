Inside Reese Witherspoons Ojai Estate The Hollywood Reporter

Inside Reese Witherspoon’s Ojai Estate – The Hollywood Reporter

  • Reese Witherspoon’s Ojai Estate

    The Hollywood Reporter exclusively confirmed that Reese Witherspoon listed her Spanish Colonial estate in Ojai, Calif., for sale at $10 million. In March, the actress married CAA talent agent Jim Toth at the Wallace Neff-designed ranch, which she's owned since 2008.

    STORY: Reese Witherspoon Picks Up 'Pennyroyal’s Princess Boot Camp'

  • The Bathroom

    Reese Witherspoon primarily lives in the Los Angeles area, and the town of Ojai — popular with entertainment industry denizens — is about a two-hour drive away.

  • The Drive-way

    Reese Witherspoon's Ojai property covers seven acres, and is known as Libbey Ranch; it includes a four-bedroom main house, which was originally designed by Wallace Neff as a barn. Also: there are horse stables, three guest cottages, a carriage house and swimming pool.

    STORY: Reese Witherspoon to Star in Romantic Comedy 'The Beard' (Exclusive)

  • The Backyard

    In a word, gorgeous. “What I love about it is that it is so period and it has so much character and charm to it,” says Billy Rose, president of The Agency, who has the $10 million listing for Reese Witherspoon's Ojai property. “There is century-old hardware and fixtures that were forged on the property — it is just so authentic.”

  • The Garden

    Reese Witherspoon bought the estate in early 2008 from prominent interior designer Kathryn Ireland, who had undertaken a renovation of the compound originally built for glassware magnate Edward Libbey circa 1923. Another fun fact: The property also has been owned by actor/director Harold Ramis.

    STORY: Reese Witherspoon Picks Up 'Pennyroyal’s Princess Boot Camp'

  • The Family Room

    Reese Witherspoon's Spanish Colonial estate went up for sale Sept. 17, several months after she and CAA talent agent Jim Toth staged their wedding on the equestrian property (which was also featured on the September cover of Elle Décor).

  • Horse Stables

    Billy Rose, president of The Agency (who has the listing, says the compound is home to a “menagerie of animals,” including horses, pigs, dogs and chickens. "It's down this country lane and very much secreted away but very much connected to the heart of Ojai."

    STORY: Reese Witherspoon Lists Ojai Estate for Sale at $10 Million
     

  • The Kitchen

    Beyond Reese Witherspoon and previous owner Harold Ramis, Libbey Ranch has another notable Hollywood connection: it doubled as Shangri-La in Frank Capra's 1937 film Lost Horizon.

  • The Living Room

    When Reese Witherspoon married CAA agent Jim Toth at her Ojai estate in March, wedding guests included: the actress' first husband Ryan Phillippe; Scarlett Johansson; Sean Penn; Alyssa Milano; Tobey Maguire; Robert Downey Jr. and Renée Zellweger.

  • The Pool

    During Reese Witherspoon’s four-year ownership tenure, Jay Griffith, a well-known Venice-based landscape architect, redesigned the grounds.
