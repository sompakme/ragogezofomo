Restoration Hardware Mansion Store Palm Beach Florida

If you've ever wanted to give your home a chic interior upgrade, chances are you've looked to Pinterest or Instagram for some inspiration. But now one-upping all of our decor mood boards is Restoration Hardware's new dream mansion store.

Located in the heart of West Palm Beach, the RH Gallery is part of the brand's new retail strategy, which focuses on creating experiences for shoppers instead of relying on traditional brick-and-mortar stores. And its latest mansion-like store is quite the experience. Not only does it serve as a mecca of interior design ideas to explore, it also boasts its own rooftop restaurant, wine vault, relaxing outdoor space, and barista bar for shoppers to enjoy.

Restoration Hardware

The rooftop restaurant at Restoration Hardware West Palm Beach

Acting as the ultimate dream home, the four-story 80,000 square foot space offers up dreamy living rooms, bedrooms, outdoor patios, and children/baby rooms to inspire. And in a time where many have predicted that online shopping will replace traditional retail stores, RH is instead looking to revolutionize the standard home shopping experience beyond e-commerce.

Restoration Hardware

"Wandering through tropical gardens featuring our expansive line of outdoor collections, and navigating three floors of interior installations while envisioning the home of your dreams with one of our interior design professionals. Dining under a soaring glass atrium with heritage olive trees and listening to the sound of trickling fountains in our rooftop restaurant. The concept of creating these homey, experiential-like spaces isn't an entirely new one for Restoration Hardware. The brand's new Palm Beach location is just the latest of mansion-like gallery stores, following locations in Boston, Chicago, Atlanta, and more.
