Where Is Brad Mcgarry Killer David Kinney Now? Everything We Know So Far!

The murder of Brad McGarry in May 2017 sent shockwaves through the small town of Bellaire, Ohio. What initially appeared as a burglary gone wrong quickly unfolded into a tale of love, betrayal, and cold-blooded murder, centering around David Kinney, a man whose actions left a community grappling with a brutal reality.

Who Is David Kinney?

David Kinney, a 37-year-old individual from Bellaire, Ohio, stood as a figure representing profound betrayal. Known initially as a loving husband and friend, Kinney’s facade fell apart following the investigation into Brad McGarry’s murder. His dual life as a married man and a secret lover was exposed, revealing the depth of his deception.

What Did David Kinney Do?

David Kinney’s act of violence on May 7, 2013, shattered the peace of Bellaire. He shot Brad McGarry, his best friend and lover, twice in the back of the head, leading to McGarry’s immediate death. The crime scene, meticulously arranged to resemble a burglary, indicated Kinney’s desperate attempt to hide his dark secret.

Kinney’s confession brought to light the complexities of his relationship with McGarry. The latter’s desire for a committed relationship conflicted with Kinney’s wish to maintain his heterosexual marriage. Kinney claimed that the murder was an act of self-defense, a claim that investigators quickly debunked. They believed that Kinney’s primary motive was to conceal his affair and his sexual orientation.

Where Is David Kinney Now?

David Kinney is currently serving a life sentence without the possibility of parole at Noble Correctional Institution in Caldwell, Ohio. His confinement marks the end of a tragic chapter and serves as a harsh reminder of the destructive potential of living a life of duplicity.
