Johnny Manziel Net Worth 2023: What Is The NFL Star Worth?

Johnny Manziel, a name synonymous with American football, has had a career filled with highs and lows. As of 2023, his net worth stands at an estimated $1 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth, a figure that reflects both his football prowess and his off-field endeavors. Manziel, often known by his nickname "Johnny Football," first rose to prominence as a phenomenal college football player at Texas A&M University. His remarkable skills on the field earned him the prestigious Heisman Trophy in 2012, making him the first freshman ever to win this award.

Manziel's transition to the NFL created significant anticipation. The Cleveland Browns drafted him in the first round of the 2014 NFL Draft, pinning their hopes on his dynamic playmaking ability. However, his NFL career didn't quite match the expectations set during his college days. His inconsistent performances and off-field controversies marred his time with the Browns. Ultimately, it led to a shorter-than-expected tenure in the league.
