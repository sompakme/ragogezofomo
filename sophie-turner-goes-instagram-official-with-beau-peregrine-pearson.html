Sophie Turner Goes Instagram-Official With Beau Peregrine Pearson

Sophie Turner is making it official with new beau Peregrine Pearson.

On Monday, the actor shared a series of photos from a recent ski trip she took with Pearson and two other friends. The four were seemingly on a couples’ getaway.

Turner and Pearson looked happy in their contrasting ski fits. The Game of Thrones star kept things colorful in a sky-blue and white checkered puffer-and-pants set, plus puffy white gloves, mirrored ski goggles, black ski boots, and a black helmet. Pearson wore an all-black look, consisting of a hooded jacket, ski pants, a helmet, goggles, and ski boots.

In one photo, Pearson is seen smiling widely while embracing Turner on the mountain, as she leans into him while wrapping her arm around her girlfriend.

In another, the couple is on a ski lift with their friends. While Pearson smiles, Turner flashes her middle finger at the camera.

“Jägerbomb anyone?” she captioned the photo dump.

The last photo in the slideshow shows a smiley Turner on the edge of an indoor pool in a teeny green thong bikini.

Turner has been dating Pearson, a British aristocrat, for a few months now. The two were first spotted kissing in late October on a trip to Paris. Earlier this month, they were seen out together again, looking cozy in each other’s company.

The romance comes just a few months after Turner and Joe Jonas, whom she shares two daughters with, announced their divorce. “After four wonderful years of marriage we have mutually decided to amicably end our marriage,” the actor and the musician said in a joint statement shared to social media. “There are many speculative narratives as to why but, truly this is a united decision and we sincerely hope that everyone can respect our wishes for privacy for us and our children.”
