Emma Jones's career in Leeds United

From June 2013 to June 2014, she worked as a freelance journalist for Channel 5 News. The following year, she relocated to Manchester to work for Bauer Media as a Traffic and Travel Reporter.

In December 2015, she left it. In January 2016, she returned to work as a radio presenter for Hull’s Viking FM and Viking 2. She was a co-host on Viking FM’s breakfast show.

She was the Signal 1 breakfast show presenter for Wireless Group Ltd from October 2017 to March 2019. In addition, she hosted Q Live from October 2017 to March 2019.
