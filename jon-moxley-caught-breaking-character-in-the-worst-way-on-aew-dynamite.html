Jon Moxley Caught Breaking Character In The Worst Way On AEW Dynamite

Jon Moxley debuted at AEW Double Or Nothing 2019 and wasted no time in making a huge impact on the company. Four years since his debut, Moxley has been on a roll in the world of professional wrestling. Moxley was part of Dynamite this week and it appears he was caught breaking character in the worst way.

This week’s Dynamite was headlined by the Blackpool Combat Club squaring off against The Elite in the main event. After The Elite won the match, Kenny Omega and Konosuke Takeshita had a fight in the ring.

It also featured Eddie Kingston returning to AEW television after being absent for three months and attacking Claudio Castagnoli, among others. However, not everything was received well by fans.

As pointed out by some eagle-eyed fans on Twitter, Jon Moxley could be seen speaking to Matt Jackson outside the ring despite the fact that they are bitter enemies as part of their ongoing storyline. Fans felt this was a bad look for the company.

We will have to wait and see how AEW will continue to book The Blackpool Combat Club and The Elite’s feud in the coming weeks, as fans are still waiting for Kota Ibushi to make his AEW debut soon.
