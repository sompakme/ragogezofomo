John Smoltz’s Early Life and Career

John Smoltz was born on May 15, 1967, in Warren, Michigan. He developed a passion for baseball at a young age and was drafted by the Detroit Tigers in 1985. Smoltz’s career took off with the Braves, where he played for two decades and became one of the most successful pitchers in the history of the franchise.
