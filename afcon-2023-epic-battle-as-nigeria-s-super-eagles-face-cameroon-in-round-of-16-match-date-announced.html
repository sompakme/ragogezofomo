AFCON 2023: Epic Battle as Nigeria's Super Eagles Face Cameroon in Round of 16, Match Date Announced

  • Nigeria will play Cameroon in the round of 16 of the AFCON 2023 at the Stade Félix Houphouët Boigny on Saturday, January 27
  • Legit.ng recalls that on Friday, January 22, Nigeria needed an own goal to beat Guinea-Bissau 1-0 and secure a place in the knockout stage of the tournament
  • On the other hand, Cameroon scored twice with time ticking away on Tuesday, January 23, to collect a dramatic 3-2 win over Gambia, to secure an AFCON last-16 place, and eliminate Ghana from the tournament

Legit.ng journalist Ridwan Adeola Yusuf has over 6-year-experience covering sports and football

Abidjan, Côte d’Ivoire - The Super Eagles of Nigeria will face the Indomitable Lions of Cameroon in the 2023 Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) round of 16.

Legit.ng reports that the fixture was confirmed after Cameroon saw off the challenge of Gambia in their last group game on Tuesday evening, January 23.

The match between Nigeria and Cameroon will take place on Saturday, January 27.

The last time the two giants met at the AFCON was in the 2019 edition in Egypt when the Super Eagles defeated the Indomitable Lions 3-2 in the round of 16 en route to their third-place finish.

Nigeria and Cameroon have met 25 times, with the former enjoying a better head-to-head record of 12 wins and 8 draws.
