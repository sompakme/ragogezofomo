AEW Adds More Sections To Full Gear Pay-Per-View

AEW Full Gear, set to take place at the Kia Forum in Inglewood, CA on November 18 at 4:00 PM local time, but the event will begin at 7:00 PM EST. The company has experienced some issues filling up venues in recent memory, and now we have some updates with additional seating options.

Wrestle Tix noted behind their paywall that AEW has added even more sections to the upper deck for Full Gear. It appears that they are making even more room to sell out the most they can in the venue.

As of the latest update, there are 2,153 individual tickets available and 228 combo tickets, while the current setup accommodates up to 11,388 attendees. A total of 9,007 tickets have been distributed, showing an increase of 130 since the last update provided four days ago.

4 new upper decks just added.

AEW apparently made further arrangements to enhance the seating capacity by adding four new upper decks for the event. These adjustments aim to accommodate more fans and enhance the overall experience for those attending AEW Full Gear on November 18th.

We will have to see how the entire event turns out. You never know what can happen in AEW, and Tony Khan really wants to fill up every seat in the house for Full Gear next month.
