Why is it called Chinese Christmas game?

Answer

People bring undesired gifts to parties, and then compete to grab the greatest games away from each other, as is the case with the game “Chinese Christmas,” according to reports. Why? Because that’s just how the Chinese are, yanno? Apparently, this game is referred to as the “White Elephant” game by others.

As a result, what exactly is the Chinese Christmas game?

The person who drew number 1 selects a gift from the pile, opens the gift, and sits down with the gift in full sight, usually on her lap, as if she were receiving it. Then “2” chooses a gift, either from the pile or from the person who gave it. Then 2 sits down with the present in front of him or her. If one’s present is stolen, one is given the opportunity to choose another from the pile.
