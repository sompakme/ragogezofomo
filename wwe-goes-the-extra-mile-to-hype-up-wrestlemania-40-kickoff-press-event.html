WWE Goes the Extra Mile to Hype Up WrestleMania 40 Kickoff Press Event

We are officially on the Road to WrestleMania 40 and the next stop is the Elimination Chamber Premium Live Event on February 24th. However, WWE has a pit stop in the form of the WrestleMania 40 kickoff press conference and it appears the company is going all out to promote it.

The Rock confronted Roman Reigns on SmackDown last week after Cody Rhodes confirmed he won’t face Reigns at WrestleMania 40, setting the stage for the next couple of months.

The Rock and The Tribal Chief are slated for a showdown at the WrestleMania 40 kickoff press conference tonight at the T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas, Nevada. Several other prominent WWE Superstars are expected to be in attendance for the event.

Former WWE writer Brian Gewirtz took to Twitter and uploaded a photo of WWE advertising the WrestleMania 40 kickoff press event in Las Vegas, as the banner was shown on a huge LED screen.

The Rock also wanted to save WrestleMania 40 with the Roman Reigns match. It remains to be seen what will transpire during the WrestleMania 40 kickoff press event.
