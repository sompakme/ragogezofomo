Ahh: Spa Montage entices with 10 days of relaxation | News, Sports, Jobs

A guest luxuriates in an outdoor treatment cabana (above). In addition to the 10 Days promo, the Be Well This Holiday Season complimentary spa event will be held Friday with an outdoor boot camp led by a lululemon ambassador, mini eye-rescue treatments, makeovers and a pop-up trunk show. Montage Kapalua Bay photo

The award-winning Spa Montage Kapalua Bay will feature the 10 Days of Holiday Relaxation with a variety of specials and offerings. The most avid spa-goers and those just seeking a respite from the holiday hassles may opt to be pampered to the nines.

So mark your calendars now. The 10 Days will kick off on Saturday, Dec. 16, with the Gift of Wellness.

Enjoy one wellness class with any 90-minute spa treatment booked. But you must experience class and service on the same day.

On Sunday, Dec. 17, get the Gift of Relaxation with the purchase of one spa facility access pass and receive one complimentary pass.

On Monday, Dec. 18, the Gift of a Brighter Complexion allows you a mask upgrade with any spa facial treatment booked.

On Tuesday, Dec. 19, the Gift of Nutrition will let you enjoy a protein boost with the purchase of any fresh-pressed juice or smoothie.

On Wednesday, Dec. 20, the Gift that Keeps on Giving lets you receive a spa holiday gift that delights with every gift card purchase.

On Thursday, Dec. 21, the Gift of Timeless Skin bestows you with a spa gift when you purchase a Timeless Swiss Booster Facial.

On Friday, Dec. 22, the Gift of Indulgence will let you revel in a conditioning scalp treatment with any barber shop service.

On Saturday, Dec. 23, the Gift of Beauty gives you 20 percent savings on any hair and makeup salon service.

On Sunday, Dec. 24, the Gift of Retail Therapy gives you 10 percent off any retail products when you reserve any facial or body treatment at Spa Montage.

On Monday, Dec. 25, the Gift of Rejuvenation buffs you out with a revitalizing scalp treatment when you get any spa treatment.

All gifts are available on the day of the offer only and may not be used on another day. It’s subject to availability, the price does not include tax and gratuity and it may not be combined with any other discounts or promotions.

You may also purchase a gift card and let the recipient choose from an array of spa services including massages, body therapies, facials, fitness classes, beauty salon, barbershop and services for children ages 4 to 15 years old. The good news is, Montage gift cards do not expire and they can be used at any Montage location for spa, dining and accommodations.

At 8 a.m. on Friday, the Be Well This Holiday Season complimentary spa event will pump you up with a full-body outdoor boot camp led by lululemon ambassador Anthony Bianucci.

“Then join us at the spa from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. to enjoy a mini Kerstin Florian Eye Rescue treatment, a La Bella Donna makeover and shop our pop-up trunk shows by Aloha Nectar Maui (jewelry) and Zenziva (scrubs and oils),” says Jennifer Holzworth, spa director.

“In our efforts to continually support local vendors and their locally-made products, Spa Montage Kapalua Bay proudly hosts a series of trunk shows throughout December. From jewelry to leather goods to clothing and beauty products, shopping for one-of-a-kind pieces is made easy at the spa or resort lobby,” Holzworth adds, describing this month’s many activities.

* For more details or for reservations, call 662-6507 or visit www.spamontagekapaluabay.com/.
