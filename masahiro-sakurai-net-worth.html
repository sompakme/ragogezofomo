Masahiro Sakurai Net Worth: The Man Behind Super Smash Bros

Early Life and Education

Masahiro Sakurai was born on August 3, 1970, in Musashimurayama, Tokyo, Japan. As a child, he was a big fan of video games and showed a remarkable talent for creating his own games from a young age. After completing high school, he attended the National College of Art and Design in Tokyo, where he honed his skills in game design and development.

Career

Sakurai’s career began in the early 1990s when he landed a job at HAL Laboratory, a video game development company in Japan. In the early 2000s, Sakurai founded his own game development company, Sora Ltd. It was during this time that he began work on what would become his most well-known creation: Super Smash Bros.
