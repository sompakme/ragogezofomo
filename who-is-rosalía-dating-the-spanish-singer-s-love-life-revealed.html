Who Is Rosala Dating? The Spanish Singers Love Life Revealed

Rosalía, at 30 years old, stands as one of the world’s most renowned and influential artists. In this blog post, we’ll delve into everything you need to know about her past and present romantic involvements.

Rosalía and Rauw Alejandro: A Dynamic Duo in Music

Currently, Rosalía is romantically linked with Rauw Alejandro, a 30-year-old Puerto Rican singer and rapper who is making waves in the Latin music scene. The confirmation of their relationship came in September 2021, putting an end to months of speculation and rumors.

The union between Rosalía and Alejandro was forged through their shared love for music. They’ve lent their talents to various collaborative tracks, such as “Dile a Él” and “La Noche de Anoche.” In March 2023, they released a joint EP titled “RR,” which featured the hit song “Beso” along with a surprising engagement announcement.

In the music video for “Beso,” their love story unfolds through clips of their travels, cuddles, and affectionate moments. Towards the video’s end, Rosalía revealed a dazzling diamond ring on her finger, expressing her love for Alejandro in Spanish.

The couple’s romantic journey is also well-documented on social media, where they share endearing photos and videos of their time together. Supporting each other’s careers and accomplishments, they often grace award shows and events as a power couple.

Rosalía and C. Tangana: A Former Flame

Before her relationship with Alejandro, Rosalía was romantically involved with C. Tangana, a 32-year-old Spanish rapper and producer, who also goes by the name El Madrileño. Their relationship spanned from 2016 to 2018, and they collaborated on several musical endeavors, such as “Antes de Morirme” and “Mala Mujer.”

Throughout their time together, Rosalía and Tangana kept their relationship largely under wraps, seldom discussing it in interviews or on social media. Their split in 2018 was reportedly due to their busy schedules and differing musical directions.

Despite parting ways romantically, they have remained close friends and artistic collaborators. They continue to praise each other’s work and express mutual respect and admiration. Their professional relationship has seen them sharing the stage, such as their performance at the 2020 Latin Grammy Awards.

Prior to her relationship with Alejandro, Rosalía was in a relationship with C. Tangana, a Spanish rapper, from 2016 to 2018. Despite keeping her love life largely private, Rosalía has found both happiness and inspiration in her romantic connections, along her remarkable career journey.
