7 summer outfit ideas you need to add to cart for 2024

    While the weather remains unpredictable, the season’s event itinerary remains animated, meaning a carefully curated selection of summer outfits is an absolute necessity. 

    From days in-office to holidays spent horizontal on a sun lounger, there’s an array of upcoming occasions that give avid shoppers the perfect excuse to zhuzh their seasonal wardrobes.

    Yet, this doesn’t require nearly the amount of effort you may think. In an age of over-consumption, the trick is to invest in versatile pieces to see you through the warmer months. Less is more, after all. 

    For example, a Pucci graphic top can be worn for coffee-dates or date-nights. A neatly tailored Connor Ives waistcoat from meeting to mooching courtside at Wimbledon. A DÔEN slip dress will transport you from Regatta to romantic European sojourn. The world is truly your oyster when it comes to summer outfit planning.

    For those with a penchant for trends, there are ample fads to lean into. The Pinterest Summer 2024 Trend Report noted a 770% spike in interest for ‘gingham shorts,’ a grand 950% interest increase for the term ‘corporate chic’ and an even more bewildering 6030%  interest in the term ‘Y3K outfits.’ 

    Ballet flats are still going strong, as are Adidas Sambas, short shorts and many fellow Gen Z-induced trends that spring to mind. 

    How to style them so they never lose their allure? Indulge in a mix of contemporary and timeless pieces, from jewellery to accessories. Look to style veterans such as Dua Lipa, Emily Ratajkowski and Sofia Richie Grainge, all of whom have mastered the art of interspersing cool-girl labels with a touch of evergreen luxury.

    Whether sporty chic or quiet luxury floats your boat (we’re currently engaged in a full-blown love affair with tropical maximalism), discover the outfits to keep on your radar for all your upcoming summer soirees.
