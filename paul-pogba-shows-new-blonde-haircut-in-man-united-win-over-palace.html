Paul Pogba shows new blonde haircut in Man United win over Palace

- Paul Pogba on Wednesday night, February 27, showed a new hairstyle in Manchester United win over Crystal Palace

- Manchester United are now occupying fifth position on the Premier League standings with 55 points

- Pogba has been impressive for the Old Trafford club since Ole Gunnar Solskjaer took over from Mourinho

Paul Pogba unleashed a new trendy blonde haircut as he walked into Selhurst Park where Manchester United defeated Crystal Palace with the Frenchman bagging an assist in the process.

One of the night's talking points was how Pogba had for the first time under interim manager Ole Gunnar Solskjaer, changed his hair to a more vibrant and expressive blonde look.

And according to Metro, United's legendary striker Eric Cantona, says the change of hair after the rise in his performance on the field, is an excellent sign that Pogba's priorities are right.

"Since Ole has arrived, he hasn’t changed his haircut. Maybe he made Pogba understand that the priority is to play football," the former United striker told Paddy Power.

Cantona said that self expression was good only if it reflected an equally good performance on the pitch.
