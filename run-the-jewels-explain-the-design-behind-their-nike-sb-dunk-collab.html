Run The Jewels Explain The Design Behind Their Nike SB Dunk Collab

In the official images which were revealed at the start of the month, you could immediately tell that these were going to be wild. Run The Jewels delivered a blue and pink offering with the Nike SB Dunk Low. Furthermore, with the Nike SB Dunk High, they opted to go crazy with the materials, while also delivering some black and pink hues. Recently, they got to like up with Nike for a special video segment in which they revealed their design philosophy. Overall, these kicks are very unique to the group.

Run The Jewels x Nike SB

In the video up above, El-P reveals that each shoe was meant to represent different eras of the group. For instance, he notes that the Nike SB Dunk Low pays homage to Run The Jewels 3. As he explains, the softer tones speak on a much moodier record. However, Run The Jewels 4 is much more in your face, which is why you get those harsh pink and black tones. Moreover, El-P goes on to speak about all of the little intricacies and how he really wanted to make these unique to the group. Overall, they accomplished that mission.

Run The Jewels x Nike SB Dunks Release Date

As it stands, these are currently on the market and they released on April 20th, 2023. Although, they have sold out in various places. However, you can get them over at GOAT and Flight Club, so be sure to check them out. Each pair is available on those platforms in an array of sizes.
