24 of Kate Middleton's best flirty red looks for Valentine's Day

The royal loves red!

    Some of Kate Middleton's most show-stopping outfits have been in fiery red, whether they're from designer labels like Alexander McQueen or high street stores such as Zara.

    The colour is traditionally associated with love, so what better way to get in the Valentine's Day spirit than by taking inspiration from some of the Duchess' best bold looks? And some of them are much more casual than you may expect…

    PHOTOS: 9 royal red-carpet looks for ultimate bridal inspiration: Kate Middleton, Princess Diana and more

    Prince William's wife's kept it casual during a visit to Peterley Manor Farm in Buckinghamshire – we love her red puffer jacket by Perfect Moment!

    In January 2020, Kate wowed us all in this glittering midi dress by high street brand Needle & Thread!

    The Duchess of Cambridge was a stand out in a red gown by Jenny Packham during the annual Diplomatic Reception at Buckingham Palace in December 2016. The Duchess finished off the elegant look with Princess Diana's Cambridge Lover's Knot Tiara.

    Looking for a statement coat, pretty skirt or warm scarf? Take inspiration from Kate's bold red outfit during her royal train tour of the UK in December 2020. She recycled her pleated tartan Emilia Wickstead skirt with her Alexander McQueen red coat.

    Who can forget the royal's ruby red frock she wore to film a festive TV special with Mary Berry for the BBC? Kate’s dress from Alessandra Rich featured long sleeves, a fitted waist and a pussybow tie, and instantly became a huge hit with royal fans. 

    During her royal tour of Sweden and Norway in 2018, the Duchess – who was pregnant with Prince Louis at the time – stunned wearing a stylish red and white coat by Catherine Walker.

    Kate was radiant in red as she joined husband Prince William and her brother-in-law Prince Harry for an engagement in London in 2017. The Duchess wore a new Giorgio Armani suit including a red fitted jacket and a co-ordinating red dress.

    Kate may have added a new designer to her already impressive wardrobe with this pretty Marchesa Notte creation, but she relied on one of her favourite colours for her evening out in April 2017.
